Phoenix

Single - 2022

In August 2022, D3 MistyProject embarked on a new era of creativity, releasing their first original track in nearly 20 years. Aptly named "Phoenix", this track symbolizes the project's rebirth, rising from the ashes to breathe new life into their unique style. This release marked the beginning of a new album, codenamed "Renaissance of the Mystery". The track "Phoenix" beautifully encapsulates the emotion of D3's early era, but with a more mature and thoughtful interpretation. It blends various styles including chillout, new age, ambient, and enigmatic, offering listeners a fresh yet nostalgic auditory experience. Released worldwide on August 13, 2022, "Phoenix" represents a pivotal moment in D3's journey, a testament to their enduring creativity and their ability to reinvent themselves while staying true to their roots.
