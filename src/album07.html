Zone Protected

2003-2004

In August 2003, a pivotal moment in D3's journey occurred with the creation of the composition "Return 2 the Alien World". This track, born amidst the completion of their previous album and the genesis of new compositions, demanded a new musical canvas and marked the inception of D3's seventh album, "Zone Protected". This album includes full versions of the short singles from "Jungle-Z-one", as well as beloved songs like "Flying Dreams 2", "5 stars for D3", and "ReZus". Conceived in 2003 and concluded in 2004, "Zone Protected" represents a significant period in D3's journey, a time when the band stepped back to reflect and recharge. Even as D3 disconnected, they did not disband. Instead, they await a reunion, a testament to their enduring bond and shared musical vision.
