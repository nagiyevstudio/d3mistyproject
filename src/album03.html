Loose Control...

2001-2002

D3's third album, "Loose Control" (2001-2002), marks a significant evolution in the group's musical journey. Initiated in 2001, this project stands out from its predecessors by embracing a more classical style, thereby defining the unique sound of D3. The album features unforgettable compositions such as "Last Millennium's Music", "On the way from India to China", and the grand "Long Expected (QUAT)", each contributing to the album's distinct character. While the classical influence was hinted at in the second project with the composition "Flying Dreams", it is "Last Millennium's Music", a song written in honor of a departing era, that truly heralds the onset of classical elements in D3's music. The lyrics of "Long Expected (QUAT)" encapsulate the album's spirit, reflecting on the passing days and the dreams and wishes that drive us, all leading to the long-expected moment of revelation.
