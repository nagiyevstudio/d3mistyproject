Journey to the Sun

EP - 2023

Introducing the new mini-album from D3 Misty Project - "Journey to the Sun". This album is being released on the popular label Cafe de Anatolia and is now available on all major music platforms, including Spotify and Apple Music today. "Journey to the Sun" is an atmospheric release that will take you on a euphoric journey through soothing soundscapes. It continues the tradition of D3 Misty Project to create music that brings listeners together and opens new horizons of sound.
Cafe de Anatolia, the label on which the album is being released, is known for its mix of music genres and the high quality of Ethno, Deep House, Chill Out, and Oriental music. Founded in 2017, Cafe de Anatolia has a multitude of albums and compilations from prominent artists, and its exclusive artists perform all around the world.
