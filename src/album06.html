junge-Z-one

2003

Sixth album is a pioneering exploration into the realm of transitional continuous music. This innovative venture marks a new chapter in D3's musical journey, distinct from their previous works, blending the familiar with the fresh. The album signifies the end of an old cycle and the dawn of a new one, introducing a novel design style and an array of new singles such as "jungleZ-Z", "My Soul", and "First ChangeZ". More than just a remixed compilation, "Jungle-Z-one" stands as a milestone in D3's discography, offering an uninterrupted auditory experience with its 30 tracks. Not all of these are full compositions, but many serve as breathers and transitions, contributing to the album's seamless flow. The narrative is framed by lyrics dedicated to the song "Long Expected", creating a cohesive journey from start to finish. The album concludes on a note of anticipation with "to be continue...", hinting at the ongoing saga and the next chapter in D3's musical journey.
