Golden Years Collection

2000 - 2021

"Golden Years Collection" signifies the revival of a forgotten project. Released in 2021, this album features the best and most sensational compositions from the project's history, now available on global music platforms.

With 31 tracks carefully chosen from a pool of 150, "Golden Years Collection" captures the energy and magic of the project's journey. Join us on this musical odyssey, as each track reflects growth and harmony.

Discover the treasure trove of musical exploration within "Golden Years Collection." Experience the album now and stay tuned for the upcoming 10th release, as we continue to push artistic boundaries.
