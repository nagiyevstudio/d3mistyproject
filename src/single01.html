Long Ago (Action 2010 Edit)

Single - 2022

In January 2022, D3 MistyProject unveiled a single that had been a decade in the making. This release, titled "Long Ago (Action 2010 Edit)", is a remix of a track from 2000, crafted in 2010, and finally released twelve years later. This single marked a significant moment in D3's journey, representing a cleansing before the advent of a new creative era. The release of this single, half a year after the ninth album compilation, is a testament to D3's commitment to their craft and their willingness to revisit and reimagine their past work. The single, which could have been part of the eighth album, lay dormant for 12 years before its release, symbolizing a return to the future. Officially released on January 15, 2022, under the label ALT Production Recordings (Nagiyev Studio), this progressive genre single is a bridge between D3's past and future.
