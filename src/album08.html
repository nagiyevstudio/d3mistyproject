TowN

2006-2010

In early 2006, D3 embarked on the creation of their eighth album, marking the end of a two-year hiatus and the beginning of a new creative phase. This period saw the birth of around ten compositions, which would be completed and added to the album over time. The album's inception was set against a backdrop of silent cities and crumbling ruins, the moon's pale glow casting a mournful tranquility over the remnants of a bygone era. As the desert encroached, erasing traces of lost beauty and power, the album began to take shape, its tracks echoing the tragic grandeur buried in time. The album, however, was a slow-burning process. The 13 tracks that eventually made up the album were written over a span of more than four years, reflecting a period of deep introspection and meticulous crafting. After the completion of this album, D3 receded from the limelight, marking another pause in their musical journey.
