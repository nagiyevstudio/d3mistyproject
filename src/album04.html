Journey 2 the New Millennium

2002

Marking a pivotal moment in D3's musical journey, the fourth album, "Journey 2 the New Millennium" (2002), is a testament to the band's continuous evolution and artistic growth. Conceived in the fertile creative period of 2002, with roots reaching back to 2001, this album is a showcase of D3's maturing sound and their commitment to pushing musical boundaries. The album's title is more than just a label; it's a reflection of its very essence, embodied in tracks like "Journey 2 the Future" and "New Millennium's Music". The album begins with "There is a Place", a composition that sets the stage for a new era, a new journey, and a new musical narrative. Born in what can be considered D3's most creatively vibrant year, "Journey 2 the New Millennium" is a cohesive work of art that encapsulates a year of musical exploration. Even as D3 continues to evolve, this album stands as a beacon of their artistic journey, offering a hopeful vision of unity and taking listeners on an immersive journey from the confines of Earth to the boundless cosmos.
