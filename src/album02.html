Fabulous Sensation

2000-2001

Continuing the journey embarked upon in their debut, D3's second album, "Fabulous Sensation" (2000-2001), is a testament to the group's innovative spirit. Conceived even before the completion of their first album, this project represents an evolution of their sound, fusing classical elements with the pulsating rhythms of drum'n'bass. The album, named after the standout single "Fabulous Inspiration", is a twin to its predecessor, sharing a creative DNA yet carving its own distinct path. Despite numerous revisions and a plethora of compositions that didn't make the final cut, "Fabulous Sensation" stands as a bold statement of D3's artistic vision. However, according to the creators, it remains a work in progress, a musical experiment that has yet to pass its beta test. This album is not just a collection of tracks, but a narrative, a story told through the universal language of music.
